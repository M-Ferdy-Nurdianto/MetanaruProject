# Project Overview: Metanaru

Metanaru is a specialized management system for idol group events, specifically designed for "METANARU" and its staff. The project focuses on a "Kawaii Metal" aesthetic—a blend of high-energy metal music culture and Japanese idol aesthetics.

## Core Purpose
- Manage fan orders for merchandise and "Cheki" (Polaroid) photos.
- Handle "OTSU" (Booth/On-The-Spot) orders during live events.
- Track member lineups and event-specific pricing.
- Provide a premium, high-aesthetic dashboard for staff and admins.

## Key Features
- **Real-time Order Sync**: Uses Supabase for instant updates when fans order.
- **Dynamic CMS**: Manage members, images, and event details.
- **Export System**: Generate Excel and PDF reports for accounting.
- **Event Lifecycle**: Manage events from "Ongoing" to "Finished" with automated backups.

## Target Users
- Staff members (Staff ERBE)
- Administrators (Admin ERBE)
- Idol members (viewing their specific data)
